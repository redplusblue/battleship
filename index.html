Battleship

Battleship is a game where you and the computer take turns guessing coordinates on a 10x10 grid. The first player to sink all of the other player's ships wins!

Instructions

  • First we place five ships on the board in horizontal or vertical directions. Ships cannot overlap. The ships are:
    • Carrier (6 spaces)
    • Battleship (4 spaces)
    • Cruiser (3 spaces)
    • Submarine (3 spaces)
    • Destroyer (2 spaces)
  • After placing the ships, the game begins. The first player to sink all of the other player's ships wins!
  • Click on spots on the computer's board to attack them. Red means a 'hit' and white means a 'miss'.
